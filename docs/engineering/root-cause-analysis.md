# Root-cause investigation template

## Observed failure

[Exact symptom, time, environment, version, and user impact.]

## Reliable reproduction

[Smallest repeatable reproduction and commands.]

## Expected versus actual behavior

[State both precisely.]

## Timeline and first incorrect state

[Identify the earliest known divergence, not only the final exception.]

## Causal chain

[Explain how the root condition produced the observed failure.]

## Evidence

[Logs, traces, metrics, queries, tests, commits, and configuration.]

## Hypotheses rejected

[Record what was tested and why it was ruled out.]

## Corrective action

[Fix the invariant or causal condition. Avoid symptom suppression.]

## Regression protection

[Tests, telemetry, alerts, validation, and documentation.]

## Follow-up

[Systemic improvements, owners, and due dates.]
