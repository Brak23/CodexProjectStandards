# Evidence design

Required evidence is the union of current policy, approved intent requirements, approved decision requirements, changed-content triggers, and plan-added strengthening.

Assign evidence to the level capable of proving the claim:

- execution evidence proves a specific attempt,
- milestone evidence proves composed behavior,
- promotion evidence proves the deployed release state,
- specialist review proves a protected domain assessment.

Evidence remains attached to the record that produced it. A replacement execution begins with an empty evidence ledger. A carry is an explicit reviewed claim against one prior evidence item and one named amendment. Milestone joint evidence cannot be carried from member execution evidence.
